The Shift We're Living Through

Think about when Arabic numerals replaced Roman numerals. It wasn't just a notation change—it made complex mathematics possible. Suddenly, problems that were impossibly tedious became tractable. Modern science emerged.

LLMs represent a similar cognitive shift. For the first time, humans can communicate with computer systems in natural language—not code, not specialized query syntax, not arcane commands. Just language.
