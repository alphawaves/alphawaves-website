Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What We Collect

When you authenticate with LinkedIn through our application, we access basic profile information (name, email, profile URL) solely to enable the integration functionality.

How We Use It

Your LinkedIn data is used only for the specific integration features you authorize. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not store your LinkedIn data on our servers.

Your Rights

You can revoke access at any time through your LinkedIn settings at linkedin.com/psettings/permitted-services.
